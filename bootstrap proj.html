Departments at ISTS Women's Engineering College

Computer Science & Engineering

Focused on emerging technologies like AI, Data Science, and Cybersecurity, the CSE department nurtures technical excellence and practical skills for industry-ready graduates.

Electronics & Communication Engineering

ECE equips students with in-depth knowledge of electronics, communication systems, VLSI, and embedded systems to solve real-world engineering challenges.

Agriculture

The Agriculture Department manages farming activities, supports farmers, and promotes modern agricultural practices.

Artificial Intelligence

The AI department focuses on machine learning, data science, and intelligent systems, enabling students to build advanced solutions that address real-world challenges using modern technologies.

VLSI

The VLSI department focuses on Very-Large-Scale Integration design and embedded systems, preparing students to design, simulate, and implement integrated circuits for modern electronic applications.



Admissions at ISTS Women's Engineering College

At ISTS Women's Engineering College, we welcome aspiring engineers passionate about innovation and learning. Our admissions are open for B.Tech programs in CSE, ECE, AI, VLSI, and Agriculture, providing a supportive environment for academic and personal growth.

Eligible candidates with intermediate (+2) or equivalent qualifications can apply for admissions through EAPCET counseling or directly at our campus.
